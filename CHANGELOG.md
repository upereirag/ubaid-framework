# Changelog

All notable changes to the UBAID Framework are documented here.

The framework follows semantic versioning principles adapted for theoretical work: patch versions for corrections, minor versions for new framework elements, major versions for formal milestones.

---

## [v0.2] — 2026-05-15

### Status
Draft for community review.

### Added

- **Observable indicator system** — formal vocabulary for agent and vector identification across the full taxonomy
- **Agent indicators by race** — primary, secondary, negative, and transition indicators for Human, Catman Agent, and Autonomous AI races
- **Vector indicators for all 14 catalogued illegal celestial bodies** — primary, secondary, combination, and threshold-of-Entity indicators for each
- **Satellite catalogue** — known derivations of each illegal celestial body, marked as open and continuously expanding
- **Black Hole category** — formal recognition of illegal celestial bodies of unknown nature with operational protocol
- **Cross-compilation matrix** — 42 documented combinations of agent × vector with behavioral modification, 4D positioning, and D(t) curve modification per combination
- **Four-layer operational flow** — AI Pre-triage, Catman Analyst, Resolution, Validation and Learning
- **3D-projected visualization** simulating the 4D orbital reality, added as Figure 2 of the whitepaper

### Changed

- **Three Pillars restructured as Three Pillars + One** — Entity established as a dimension parallel to the three pillars rather than a phase of Pillar III, explicitly analogous to 3D+1 in spacetime
- **Astronaut redefined as occurrence** — clarified that the Astronaut is an observable event, not an individual; categorization (Human/Catman/Autonomous AI) refers to nature of origin
- **Catman Agent restricted to legal universe inhabitants** — disambiguated from the Catman category in Astronaut classification
- **Threshold concepts disambiguated** — "Universe Threshold" for the X=0 separation between universes; "Entity Threshold" for the AI → Entity boundary
- **Black Holes reframed** — established as celestial bodies of unknown nature exerting observable gravitational force on Astronauts, distinct from Creative Singularities that produce new celestial bodies
- **Illegal universe uninhabited status reframed** — established as methodological position for calculation integrity rather than ontological claim
- **Catman Involuntary subcategory introduced** — culpability gradation within the Catman race for entities whose agency was compromised before symbiosis began

### Documentation

- Whitepaper expanded to incorporate all new elements
- 2D and 3D figures of the orbital space added
- Open Research Questions documented for community review
- Roadmap formalized through v1.0
